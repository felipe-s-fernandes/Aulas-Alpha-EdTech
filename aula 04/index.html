Trails from Zero appreciation page!

This page was made in order to spread propaganda for the Legend of Heroes' franchise, mainly the newest title in the series: Trails from Zero

Here's a list of reasons why you should play Trails from Zero:

  • Awesome JRPG turn-based and grid-based combat;
  • Great story and characters;
  • The best music in the series;
  • You get to play Trails to Azure right after;
  • KeA is in the game.
